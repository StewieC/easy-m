Getting Started

Welcome to easy-m! This app helps users manage group contributions, payouts, and group finances seamlessly. To get started:

  • Log in or create an account to access the dashboard.
  • Join or create a group to begin managing contributions.

Dashboard

The dashboard is your main hub. From here, you can:

  • View the groups you are a member of.
  • Click on a group to view its details, including balances and payouts.
  • Access options to join or create new groups.

Creating a Group

To create a new group:

  1. Click the "Create Group" button on the dashboard.
  2. Provide the group name, weekly contribution amount, interest rate, and payout cycle length.
  3. Click "Submit" to create the group. You will automatically be set as the group admin.

Joining a Group

To join an existing group:

  1. Click the "Join Group" button on the dashboard.
  2. Enter the unique group code provided by the group's admin.
  3. Click "Join" to become a member of the group.

Making a Contribution

To contribute to a group:

  1. Go to the group’s details page.
  2. Click the "Contribute" button.
  3. Enter the amount you want to contribute and click "Submit".
  4. The app will redirect you to a payment gateway to complete the transaction.

Understanding Payouts

Payouts are distributed based on the group’s payout cycle settings. To view details about payouts:

  • Go to the group’s details page to see the next recipient, date, and expected payout amount.
  • Payouts are scheduled on Sundays at 8:00 AM, following alphabetical order of members.

Viewing Contribution History

To view a group’s contribution history:

  1. Go to the group’s details page.
  2. Click the "View Contribution History" button.
  3. You’ll see a detailed table of contributions, including member names, amounts, and dates.

Group Management

Admins can manage their groups by:

  • Generating join codes for new members.
  • Adding or removing members (except the admin).
  • Viewing and managing contributions and payouts.
